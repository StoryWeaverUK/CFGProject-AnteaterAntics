What are the various species of anteater?

There are four different species of anteater. The giant anteater, norther and southern tamandua, and the silky anteater. All four species are found in the order Pilosa, which also contains sloths. The anteaters are divided into two different families, Cyclopedidae and Myrmecophagidae.
